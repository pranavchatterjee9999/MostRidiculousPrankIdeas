Ridiculous But Harmless Pranks

Colored Milk

Add a few drops of food coloring to a milk carton. Watch your family’s face when they pour green cereal milk!

Upside-Down Keyboard

Carefully pop off keys and reattach them upside down or rearranged. Works best on hunt-and-peck typists!

Frozen Cereal

Pour cereal and milk in a bowl and freeze it overnight. Serve it next morning like nothing’s wrong.
